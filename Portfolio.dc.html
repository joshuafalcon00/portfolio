hi — i'm Joshua, and I make stories feel effortless.

I'm Joshua, a fresh Education graduate and video editor with 5 years of experience, now also working as a virtual assistant. I edit scroll-stopping videos for social media like Reels, TikToks, YouTube, and promotional ads, and handle back-office tasks that keep businesses organized and running smoothly. I also have foundational bookkeeping skills in QuickBooks Online gained through hands-on simulations.

I work best independently, thriving in non-voice, task-driven environments where accuracy and consistency matter. I take pride in delivering work on time, keeping things organized, and making sure every client walks away satisfied. Whether it's a tight deadline or a detailed data entry task, I show up with integrity and get it done.
